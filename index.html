About me

Hi, I'm Alex Buck and I like maps! I grew up in New Hampshire, went to college in Montana, joined the Navy and was stationed in Virginia. Now, I'm a Planner for Tidewater Planning & Design specializing in providing planning and engineering service to the federal government. I enjoy making maps of all types and exploring the intricacies of life here on Spaceship Earth.

When I'm not fiddling with maps or working, you can usually find me outdoors. I enjoy spending time with my family and exploring and experiencing new places with them. I'm an avid golfer, skier, hiker and all around neature lover.
